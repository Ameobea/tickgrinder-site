TickGrinder

A low-latency algorithmic trading platform written in Rust.

TickGrinder is a fully-featured algorithmic trading platform that covers all the stages of the developing, testing, and deploying your own trading algorithms. It was designed with Foreign Exchange in mind but is capable of being extended to integrate with virtually any market including stocks and crpytocurrencies.

Being written in Rust, the platform gains all the extensive safety features as well as the native-level performance required for low-latency algorithms and strategies.

The platform comes ready out-of-the-box to accomplish a wide variety of tasks. Everything is accessible through the MM (Management and Monitoring) web interface allowing you to keep track of the whole platform from one location.

Integrated Data Collection

You can download, process, analyze, and transfer historical data directly from the web interface. The platform comes pre-loaded with a working integration to the native FXCM API, allowing you to download years of high-quality tick data easily.

Advanced Backtesting

There are many options to fine-tune your backtests in order to represent the real market as accurately as possible. The platform contains a complete simulated broker to replicate results at tick-level accuracy.

Easy Extensibility

On the inside, the platform attempts to keep as many things as possible generic and well-documented, making it easy to add new features. Brokers, Data sources, Indicators, and more can be added by simply implementing a trait.
